My Favorite Band

My favorite band is The Beatles. They are a rock band from the United Kingdom from a city called Liverpool in 1960. The band’s members are Paul McCartney, John Lennon, George Harrison and Ringo Starr. They are considered to be the most successful band in the world as they influence many of the music we listen today. They lasted only nine years from 1960 to 1969. My favorite song is “Hey Jude” by them.
